Welcome To Purple Cane Tea Cuisine 紫藤茶原

Purple Cane Tea Cuisine is uniquely prepared, infusing tea in every dish to bring out the natural aroma and flavor of food. As a flavor enhancer, tea adds a subtle and refined taste.

Every dish is inspired by the traditions of tea culture and developed around recipes using different types of tea. All our dishes are low-salt, low-fat, and low-seasoning, which allow you to enjoy great delicious food in a wholesome way.
