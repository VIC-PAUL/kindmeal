Welcome To The Black Cat Cafe

Black Cat Cafe offers flavors from around the world, natural, healthy and fresh. Breads, pizza dough, pasta are handmade by our accomplished chef from Canada. We serve a variety of egg- free home made cakes and pastries.

Ambience is warm and homely surrounded by books, art pieces and musical instruments. We strive to keep our prices affordable to enable people to appreciate vegetarian food and in the process hope to save some animals
